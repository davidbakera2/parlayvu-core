# Christ's Hope Client Brief

Client: Christ's Hope International

Project: Interview Video Parlay — turn interview footage into a steady stream of social media posts, shorts, and supporting assets across channels.

Mission context:

- Christ's Hope International empowers orphaned and vulnerable children to break the cycles of extreme poverty, AIDS, and spiritual brokenness through family-based care and discipleship in Jesus Christ.
- All public storytelling centers the children and the local family-based care model. Donors, partners, and sponsors are secondary characters in those stories, not the lede.

Brand voice:

- Warm, mission-driven, plain-spoken.
- Lead with story, not statistics. Use numbers only to anchor a story.
- Never corporate, never glossy.
- Faith-rooted but accessible to non-faith readers and donors.
