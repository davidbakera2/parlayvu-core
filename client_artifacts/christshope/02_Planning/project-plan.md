# Christ's Hope Project Plan

Working lanes:

- Planned: upcoming interview shoots, storytelling cycles, and campaign moments.
- In Progress: active production work by agents (Alex visuals, Ava copy, Riley publishing) or humans on the CH side.
- Needs Approval: outputs blocked on CH sign-off (consent, donor-facing claims, copy).
- Published / Complete: approved and shipped work.

Initial working set:

- Inbound interview footage pipeline — source, log, store with consent metadata.
- Per-interview parlay kit — long-form cut + 3 Reels + 5 social posts + supporting copy.
- Donor / partner storytelling cadence — TBD with the CH team.
- Approval gate for any child-featuring content and donor-facing claims.
- Lightweight performance snapshot (engagement, reach, growth) once channels start producing data.

Milestone checklist (first 30 days):

- Confirm CH point of contact and approval workflow.
- Inventory existing interview footage and identify the first parlay candidate.
- Confirm consent documentation status for the first piece of footage.
- Produce the first interview parlay kit end-to-end as a process test.
- Get CH sign-off on tone, format, and rhythm of the first kit.
- Establish weekly working-meeting cadence with Nathan and the CH lead.
- Stand up the performance snapshot template with whatever data is available.
