# RamAir Project Plan

Working lanes:

- Planned: upcoming interviews, episodes, campaigns, and deliverables.
- In Progress: active production work by agents or humans.
- Needs Approval: outputs blocked on client or internal decisions.
- Published / Complete: approved and shipped work.

Initial working set:

- Current podcast episode campaign.
- Upcoming interview or event.
- Weekly campaign kit deliverable.
- Approval gate for publishing/deployment.
- Lightweight performance snapshot.

Milestone checklist:

- Confirm current source episode and transcript.
- Confirm upcoming interview/event details.
- Generate weekly campaign kit.
- Request approval for client-facing claims, publishing, and deployment.
- Record published assets.
- Update performance snapshot with sourced metrics only.
